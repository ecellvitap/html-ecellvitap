Who Are We

We are the Entrepreneurial Cell of VIT-AP University, a dynamic and driven student organization committed to nurturing the spirit of entrepreneurship on our campus. Our mission is to provide a platform for students to explore and develop their business ideas, connect with like-minded peers, and gain valuable skills and knowledge in the world of startups and innovation.

Club Members

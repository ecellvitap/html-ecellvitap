Who Are We

We are the Entrepreneurial Cell of VIT-AP University, a dynamic and driven student organization committed to nurturing the spirit of entrepreneurship on our campus. Our mission is to provide a platform for students to explore and develop their business ideas, connect with like-minded peers, and gain valuable skills and knowledge in the world of startups and innovation.

Club Members

Welcome to our college's Entrepreneurial Cell ! Our team consists of dynamic students from various disciplines, each bringing their unique talents and perspectives to the table. Our Upcoming Events

Exciting events are on the horizon! From inspiring guest speakers to networking mixers and hands-on sessions, we’ve lined up a range of activities designed to spark creativity and drive success. Dive into our upcoming events and find opportunities to learn, connect, and grow with fellow members. Stay tuned and get ready to make the most of these fantastic opportunities!

Meet And Greet

Kick off your college journey with our Freshers Meet & Greet! Connect with new arrivals, meet seasoned members, and explore how our club can fuel your entrepreneurial dreams. Enjoy an evening of inspiration, networking, and opportunity!

Business Conclave

An exceptional day dedicated to insightful discussions, networking, and idea exchange with industry leaders. Dive into a range of panels, keynotes, and workshops designed to inspire innovation and foster professional growth.Don’t miss this chance to elevate your professional journey!

CXO Round Table

The CXO’s Round-Table is crafted to boost your business acumen through engaging, interactive learning and creative teamwork. Gain valuable feedback, expand your network, and build essential skills to excel professionally. Join us for a supportive and enriching experience to refine your expertise and confidently tackle future challenges..

Startup Weekend

Startup Weekend compresses the entrepreneurial journey into 54 action-packed hours. Participants pitch ideas, form teams, and refine concepts with expert mentorship. Through intense iteration, prototyping, and strategic planning, teams prepare for final pitches. Regardless of the outcome, the experience gained fosters confidence and sets the stage for successful startup ventures.

Monopoly

join us for an exciting Monopoly event where players buy, trade, and build to win! Experience thrilling strategy, unexpected twists, and fierce competition. It’s a fun, dynamic challenge for all ages

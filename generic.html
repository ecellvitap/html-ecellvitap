About Us

The Entrepreneurship Club offers a valuable opportunity for students to enhance their entrepreneurial skills, ultimately improving their capacity to excel in events highlighting business and finance . By providing engaging sessions,events and seminars, the Entrepreneurship Club assists students in upskilling within the entrepreneurial domain. This proactive approach not only ensures effective participation but also elevates the caliber of students engaging in competitions. Consequently, the Entrepreneurship Club plays a pivotal role in fostering a high-quality entrepreneurial ecosystem within the student community.
